California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and Other Privacy Rights

InsuranceAutoWorld.com respects your privacy rights and provides you with control over your personal information. This page explains how you can exercise your right to opt out of the sale of your personal information.

Your Right to Opt Out

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and similar state laws, you have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information to third parties. When you submit a request to opt out, we will stop selling your personal information to third parties, subject to certain exceptions permitted by law.

What Information We Collect and Share

InsuranceAutoWorld.com collects various types of personal information from users of our website, including:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number, postal address)
  • Vehicle information
  • Demographic information
  • Insurance history and preferences
  • Information about your use of our website

We may share this information with insurance providers, marketing partners, and service providers to help connect you with insurance options and improve our services.

Additional Privacy Rights

In addition to the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information, you may also have the following rights depending on your state of residence:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect about you
  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our privacy team at privacy@insuranceautoworld.com.

Response Timeframe

We will respond to your request within 45 days of receipt. If we require more time, we will inform you of the reason and extension period in writing.

Verification Process

To protect your privacy and security, we may need to verify your identity before processing your request. We will use the information you provide in this form to verify your identity.
